Responsive, SEO Compliant and Fully Optimised

With a plethora of ready-to-go E-Commerce solutions, the key consideration while building a new website isn't just about getting it up and running. We're experts in helping you get your E-Commerce website up and running the right way - as a responsive, discoverable and powerful sales tool that your business to be found and provides users with a great experience once they're on your website.

Fully Responsive

E-Commerce websites can no longer be limited to just desktops. Users today want and more importantly, expect to be served on all platforms including Desktops, Tablets and Smartphones. All E-Commerce product built by us are designed and developed with a Mobile First approach. Given that audiences today are more likely to shop on their smartphones, an effective responsive experience is critical to any E-Commerce website that aims to convert. However, making sure that your website is responsive is just a part of E-Commerce strategy.

Discoverable

Discoverability is just as important. Search Engine Optimisation or SEO is the product of an optimised website coupled with optimised content. At Brand Catalyst Media, we understand and ensure that your store website is fully SEO compliant and ready to be discovered once populated with the right content. We ensure that our coding standards follow the most current, recommended and accepted guidelines for SEO friendly development including effective content architectures, navigation patterns, W3C compliance and page speed optimisation - all of which directly impact a website's SERP. As with all our website development solutions, stores, E-Commerce websites and platforms built by us are fully SEO ready with realtime sitemaps, dynamic meta tagging, canonical friendly URLs and social media sharing (Open Graph tags, Twitter cards). We also ensure that we follow Google's Structured Data patterns to help you get the most out of your website.

Performant

While we're on the subject of optimisation, here's some food for thought. We're so serious about optimisation that our average performance score (GTMetrix) is 95% on all websites. Simply put, the web should be fast and the fact is that slower websites have a lower search engine ranking. We help you address performance with a passion.
